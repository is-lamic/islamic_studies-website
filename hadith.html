What is Hadith?

Hadith refers to the sayings, actions, and approvals of the Prophet Muhammad (peace and blessings be upon him). It serves as a key source of Islamic teachings alongside the Quran, helping Muslims understand and apply divine guidance.

Major Hadith Collections

The six major Hadith collections (Kutub al-Sittah) are considered the most authentic and influential books in Sunni Islam:

  • Sahih Bukhari — Collected by Imam Bukhari
  • Sahih Muslim — Collected by Imam Muslim
  • Sunan Abu Dawood — Collected by Abu Dawood
  • Jami' at-Tirmidhi — Collected by Imam Tirmidhi
  • Sunan an-Nasa'i — Collected by An-Nasa'i
  • Sunan Ibn Majah — Collected by Ibn Majah
